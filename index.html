How to set up Hubtel SMS
1️⃣ Go to hubtel.com and create a Business account
2️⃣ Navigate to SMS → API Access and generate your Client ID & Client Secret
3️⃣ Register a Sender ID (your station name, e.g. FuelStation) — takes 1–2 business days to approve
4️⃣ Top up your Hubtel wallet with GHS to send messages
5️⃣ Paste your credentials above, enter your phone number, save & send a test SMS to confirm it works
⚠️ SMS sending requires a backend proxy in production due to browser CORS restrictions. Use the sandbox mode on hubtel.com for local testing.
